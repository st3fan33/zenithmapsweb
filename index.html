Romanian Mountain Maps

Complete coverage of the Carpathian mountain ranges

Făgăraș Mountains

Complete coverage of Romania's highest range featuring Moldoveanu Peak (2544m), Negoiu, and all major trails through the spectacular alpine terrain.

Bucegi Mountains

Detailed maps of Bucegi featuring Omu Peak, Babele, Sphinx, Caraiman Cross, and all cable car routes from Sinaia and Bușteni.

Retezat National Park

Maps of Retezat's pristine wilderness, glacial lakes (Bucura, Zănoaga), Peleaga Peak, and the complete network of mountain trails.

Piatra Craiului Ridge

Specialized maps for the dramatic limestone ridge, featuring Zărnești Gorge, La Om peak, and the challenging crest traverse route.

Parâng & Cindrel Mountains

Coverage of the Parâng massif with Parângul Mare (2519m) and the Cindrel range near Sibiu, including ski resort areas.

Apuseni & Rodna Mountains

Maps of Apuseni's karst formations and caves, plus Rodna Massif with Pietrosu Peak and the spectacular Borșa region.

About ZenithMaps

Your guide to the Romanian Carpathians

We specialize in creating detailed, accurate topographic maps of Romania's mountain ranges. From the towering peaks of Făgăraș to the limestone ridges of Piatra Craiului, our maps help you safely explore the Carpathians.

All maps feature Romanian mountain rescue points (Salvamont), mountain huts (cabane), marked trails with official colors, water sources, and emergency information.
